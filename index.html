Frequently Asked Questions

What is included in a deep clean?

A deep clean includes detailed cleaning of all rooms, inside appliances, baseboards, window sills, and all hard-to-reach areas. We also clean bathrooms and kitchens thoroughly.

Do I need to be home during the cleaning?

No, you don't need to be home. Many of our clients provide access codes or keys. We are fully insured and bonded for your peace of mind.

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend booking at least one week in advance, though we often have availability for sooner appointments. Members get priority scheduling.

Do you bring your own supplies?

Yes, we bring all cleaning supplies and equipment. If you have specific products you prefer, just let us know and we'll use those instead.
